About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Samantha Considine, but I usually go by Sam. I have lived in southern New Jersey my whole life and currently reside in Runnemede. I work at the University of Penn as an Audiovisual Technician with Wharton Computing.

I am an avid Philadelphia sports fan and especially love the Eagles and the Flyers. I like to cook in my spare time and have two cats named Pickle and Beans.
